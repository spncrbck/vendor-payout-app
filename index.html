About consignment rates

The consignment rate is what percentage of net sales (after tax and processing fees) goes to the vendor. 70% means they receive 70% and Alpenglow keeps 30% as commission. Set to 100% for vendors like Methow Candles who get all proceeds minus only processing fees.
